Spanish Farro

  1. Spanish Farro
  • Time: 1 h 10 m
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
tablespoons butter 2
cup finely diced onions 1/2
Olice oil cooking spray
chicken broth 2 cups
pearled farro, rinsed, drained well 2 cups
tomato paste

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)..

Step 2: Grill bell peppers, turning occasionally, until softened and charred in spots, about 8 minutes.

Step 3:Melt butter in a heavy skillet with a tight-fitting lid over medium heat. Add onions and salt; cook and stir until onions have softened up and turned translucent, about 5 minutes. Add cumin, ancho chili powder, and chipotle chili powder; cook and stir about 1 minute. Add tomato paste; cook and stir about 2 minutes over medium heat. Add oregano and drained farro. Stir until farro is completely coated with the spice/butter mixture, about 2 minutes.

Step 4:Pour in chicken broth. Raise heat to high and bring to a simmer. As soon as mixture starts to bubble, stir, and cover. Remove from heat.

Step 5:Place pan in preheated oven and bake until farro is tender, 50 to 60 minutes. Fluff grains with a fork.

Enjoy!

Hearty Vegetable Lasagna

  1. Hearty Vegetable Lasagna
  • Time: 1 h 40 m
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
lasagna noodles 1 (16 ounce) package
cup finely diced onions 1/2
Olice oil cooking spray
chicken broth 2 cups
shredded mozzarella cheese 2 cups
tomato paste

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)..

Step 2: Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling water for 10 minutes, or until al dente. Rinse with cold water, and drain..

Step 3:In a large saucepan, cook and stir mushrooms, green peppers, onion, and garlic in oil. Stir in pasta sauce and basil; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes.

Step 4:Pour in chicken broth. Raise heat to high and bring to a simmer. As soon as mixture starts to bubble, stir, and cover. Remove from heat.

Step 5:Layer 1/2 each, lasagna noodles, ricotta mix, sauce, and Parmesan cheese. Repeat layering, and top with remaining 2 cups mozzarella cheese.

Step 6:Bake, uncovered, for 40 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.

Enjoy!
